Learn About Us

Namayiana is a non-profit charity organization

  • About
  • Mission
  • Vision
Namayiana is a non-profit charity organization that is committed to creating positive change in vulnerable communities around the world. Our organization is dedicated to empowering individuals and communities to break the cycle of poverty and improve their quality of life.
The mission of Namayiana Charity is to empower and uplift vulnerable communities in Narok County by creating sustainable programs and initiatives that promote social and economic development, provide access to basic needs, and improve the quality of life for those in need. We believe in working in partnership with local communities to design and implement programs that are tailored to meet their unique needs, with a focus on promoting education, skills training, and entrepreneurship.
The vision of Namayiana Charity is to create a world where every individual has access to basic needs, education, and opportunities to thrive. We envision a future where marginalized and disadvantaged communities have the resources and support they need to break the cycle of poverty and build a brighter future for themselves and future generations. We aim to empower individuals and communities to become self-sufficient, confident, and resilient, creating a positive ripple effect that extends far beyond the communities we serve. Our ultimate goal is to create a world where everyone can reach their full potential and contribute positively to society.

What We Do?

We believe that we can save more lifes with you

Healthy Food

At Namayiana, we understand the importance of healthy food and its impact on overall health and wellbeing. That's why we strive to promote healthy eating habits and provide access to healthy food options in the communities we serve.

Pure Water

We believe that access to clean and safe water is a fundamental human right. Unfortunately, many people around the world do not have access to pure water and suffer from waterborne illnesses that can be life-threatening.

Health Care

We believe that everyone should have access to quality healthcare, regardless of their economic or social status. Unfortunately, many people in our communities are unable to afford the medical care they need, putting their health and well-being at risk.

Primary Education

We believe that every child deserves the opportunity to receive a quality education. Primary education is the foundation of a child's development and sets them on the path towards a brighter future.

Residence Facilities

We believe that everyone deserves a safe and comfortable place to call home, regardless of their financial situation or circumstances.

Social Care

We believe that everyone deserves to live a life that is fulfilling and meaningful, and that's why we are committed to providing social care to those who need it the most.
